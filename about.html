I'm Emilio, aka Eyes of a Nomad. 

Photography has always been a passion in my life, starting when I was a toddler, grabbing my father’s camera, emptying his entire roll of film aimlessly in a few short minutes and loving every minute of it. However, it wasn’t until I left my home in Melbourne, Australia, & began to travel the world, that my love for capturing a fraction of a second, something breathtakingly beautiful or a little crazy became an obsession.

I am now a full time photographer, content creator & mentor living the dream in beautiful Bali, Indonesia. 

If I’m not helping brands capture the essence of their personality and telling their story through my photography/videography, I’m travelling the world or teaching the next generation of content creators through my school the Institute of Code .

My career has seen me shooting fashion editorials, celebrities, fine dining restaurants & epic hotels around the world. My work has been seen in the NY Times and I’ve had the pleasure to be interviewed by major news networks, such as BBC & the Discovery Channel. 

Beyond my work as a photographer, I also run a creative agency (Kelapa Creative) that offers full shoot management, including logistic such as management of models & HUMA, to location scouting & styling to helping with the initial creative direction & shoot vision. We also offer web development (incl shopify), social media management, branding & marketing consultation. What ever the vision, we can make it happen.
