Eyes of a Nomad.

Fashion Lifestyle Product Venue Food

I am a photographer, content creator & photography mentor living the dream in beautiful Bali, Indonesia.

If I’m not helping brands capture the essence of their personality and telling their story through my photography/videography, I’m travelling the world or teaching the next generation of content creators through my school the Institute of Code.

Who.

Photography has always been a passion in my life, starting when I was a toddler, grabbing my father’s camera, emptying his entire roll of film aimlessly in a few short minutes and loving every minute of it. However, it wasn’t until I left my home in Melbourne, Australia, & began to travel the world, that my love for capturing a fraction of a second, something breathtakingly beautiful or a little crazy became an obsession.
